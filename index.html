More Than Meets the Eye

Like many people, I love a good story. One with detail, one with direction, and most importantly, one with meaning. Unlike many people however, I also love a good story told by data. Too often people find data boring, but I’m a believer that it shouldn’t be that way; the story simply just isn't being told well most of the time.

I'm here to flip this problem on its head.

I seek to use my experience with data engineering, system design, and data analysis to help rewrite the narrative and be a positive force in making data accessible and usable for all. Because after all, every good data-based story needs to start with good foundation of data and database design.
