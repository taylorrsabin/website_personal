About Me

Snapshot

I am an experienced data engineer, consultant, and support specialist, with a passion for data. Vast troves of it exist already, and it is being recorded in ever increasing amounts. I believe that the understanding and use of data has almost infinite possibilities today, and I am highly motivated to continue to discover them for the betterment of you, me, and the world around us. I have experience with database management and engineering using SQL and Informatica; web development with HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, and python-django; and data analysis with Microsoft Azure, python, and Tableau. I have a strong background in database management and technical support, using my statistical, mathematical, and technical expertise to serve those around me. Currently, I am working towards a Bachelor's degree in Information Systems from Brigham Young University.

Personal Life

I was born and raised in a small town called Gig Harbor, Washington. Located just an hour southwest of Seattle, I grew up in a small, ocean-front town that was surrounded by greenery and beauty that is unmatched. I spent most of my childhood outdoors, which included camping, hiking, cycling, and running just to name a few. I was fortunate enough to have been raised into a wonderful family of five and I will always be grateful for the support and many opportunities that I was provided. In school, the subjects of math and science were always my favorite, and after enrolling as a student at Brigham Young University, I knew that I ought to pursue a career in a related field. After exploring many different STEM majors, I came across the Information Systems program and quickly realized that this was the path for me. I anticipate graduating in the year 2023 and I am particularly interested in the field of data engineering and science. I love almost anything that involves being in the outdoors! During high school, I participated in cross country and road cycling, both of which I still enjoy doing regularly. I also enjoy camping and hiking, and I have most recently spent the most time rock climbing. In my free time, I like to play the acoustic and bass guitar with friends.
